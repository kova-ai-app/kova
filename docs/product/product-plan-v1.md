# Kova — Product Plan (v1)
## Revenue Intelligence for Drain & Plumbing

---

## 1. Product Definition

**Kova** is a mobile-first revenue intelligence platform for drain and plumbing businesses.

It records service calls, analyzes technician behavior, and identifies **missed revenue opportunities** in every job.

> **Core Promise:**  
> “We show you exactly where your team is losing money—per call.”

---

## 2. Target Customer

### Primary
- Drain & plumbing companies
- 2–15 technicians
- Owner-operators or small teams

### Design Partner
- Drain Right (California)

---

## 3. Core Use Case

Technicians and sales reps perform in-home service calls.

Kova:
1. Records the interaction
2. Transcribes the conversation
3. Analyzes behavior using trade-specific logic
4. Outputs:
   - Missed revenue
   - Coaching insights
   - Performance tracking

---

## 4. Core Workflow

### Step 1: Record
- Mobile app records service call
- Offline capable
- Consent captured (CA compliant)

### Step 2: Analyze
- Transcription
- Rule-based scoring engine
- Opportunity detection

### Step 3: Output

#### Example:
**Missed Revenue: $3,850**

Breakdown:
- No camera inspection offered (+$450)
- No hydrojet upgrade presented (+$900)
- No maintenance plan offered (+$2,500 LTV)

### Step 4: Coach
- 1–2 actionable insights
- Clip-based feedback

### Step 5: Track
- Owner dashboard:
  - Revenue per call
  - Missed revenue per tech
  - Opportunity capture rate

---

## 5. Core Differentiation

### Not a coaching tool
### Not conversation intelligence

> **Kova = Revenue Intelligence Engine for service calls**

Key differences:
- Technician-first (not just sales reps)
- Trade-specific scoring (plumbing + drain)
- Revenue modeling (not just scores)

---

## 6. Scoring System (v1)

### A. Drain Call Model

Phases:
1. Problem intake
2. Diagnosis
3. Root cause explanation
4. Solution presentation
5. Upsell opportunities
6. Close

---

### B. Drain Scoring Dimensions

#### Diagnosis Quality
- Root cause explained
- Recurrence risk discussed

#### Camera Inspection
Triggers:
- “This keeps happening”
- “We had someone out before”

Missed if not offered

---

#### Hydrojet vs Snaking
- Long-term vs short-term explained

---

#### Maintenance Plan
- Offered and tied to customer pain

---

#### Customer Education
- Built trust vs rushed to price

---

#### Close Quality
- Options presented vs single quote

---

### C. Plumbing Scoring Dimensions

#### Whole-Home Walkthrough
- Checked additional fixtures

---

#### Water Heater Opportunity
Triggers:
- Age mentioned
- Rusty water
- Slow recovery

---

#### Filtration / Softener
- Mentioned vs ignored

---

#### Fix vs Improve Framing
- Upsell beyond immediate issue

---

## 7. Missed Revenue Engine

### How It Works

#### Step 1: Detect Signals
- Keywords
- Context
- Timing

#### Step 2: Map to Opportunity
- Water heater → $2k–$6k
- Camera inspection → $200–$500
- Hydrojet → $400–$1k

#### Step 3: Output Estimate

> “Missed Revenue: $X–$Y”
